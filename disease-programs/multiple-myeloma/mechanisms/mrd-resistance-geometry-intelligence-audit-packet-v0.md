## Purpose

This packet is the Phase 3 internal adversarial audit for the MRD Resistance
Geometry Pilot.

It replaces external human outreach with a stricter internal loop:

- source-trace audits
- contradiction hunts
- axis-collapse pressure tests
- claim-boundary red teams
- coverage-count misuse checks
- remaining-gap routing
- no-human-outreach compliance checks

The goal is not to approve the geometry. The goal is to attack it hard enough
that the next axis revision either survives with narrower wording or gets
split, weakened, or blocked.

## Boundary

- Research-use only.
- Not medical advice.
- Not diagnostic guidance.
- Not a treatment recommendation.
- Not a monitoring recommendation.
- Not a trial recommendation.
- Not a patient-specific MRD interpretation.
- Not a mechanism ranking.
- Not a cure claim.
- No external human outreach is required or scheduled.
- Internal audit status is not clinical validation.

## Attack Questions

### Measurement Versus State

- Does any artifact collapse NGS or flow MRD measurement trajectory into
  single-cell residual-cell biology?
- Are assay method, specimen, threshold, sampling time, and response context
  preserved before comparison?
- Would a reader mistake MRD trajectory labels for patient-specific
  interpretation?

### Clone-State Coupling

- Does clone-state coupling overstate causality from source-specific
  single-cell observations?
- Do clone identity and transcriptional state remain separate fields?
- Does subclone diversity need more branching before the edge is named more
  strongly?

### Pathway Overreach

- Does any pathway signal become a treatment, target, supplement, or monitoring
  suggestion?
- Are metabolic, NF-kB, hypoxia, protein-folding, and mitochondrial-respiration
  signals marked as research-map signals only?
- Are the metabolic and NF-kB second-source gaps still visible?

### Coverage Misuse

- Does `covered-for-v0-navigation` imply stronger evidence, higher frequency,
  or clinical readiness anywhere?
- Are under-covered buckets protected from being treated as lower biological
  priority?
- Are the remaining six second-source gaps still visible?

### Contradiction Mining

- What sources would weaken or contradict each covered edge?
- Which assay, cohort, therapy, or timing mismatch could make two sources
  non-comparable?
- Which currently covered edge is most likely to split after contradiction
  mining?

### Clinical Boundary

- Could any wording be read as prognosis, treatment selection, monitoring
  guidance, trial guidance, or cure claim?
- Does the phrase cure-oriented remain clearly distinct from claiming cure?
- Does no-human-outreach mode accidentally remove scientific caution?

## Completion Gate

- Every audit item has a pass, fail, or blocked status before
  `mrd-resistance-geometry-axis-revision-v0` is created.
- Every pass decision cites source IDs and extraction record IDs.
- Every fail decision produces one of: weaken wording, split edge, add gap, add
  contradiction-mining task, or block claim upgrade.
- No external human outreach is required or scheduled.
- No cure, prognosis, treatment, monitoring, diagnostic, or trial guidance is
  introduced.
