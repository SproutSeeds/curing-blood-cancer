# Public Safety

This repo is for public blood-cancer research tooling and education. It is not
a medical device, clinician, diagnostic service, or treatment recommendation
engine.

Stewardship mark: [frg.earth](https://frg.earth/).

## Safety Rules

- Do not publish patient-specific advice.
- Do not imply a blood-cancer cure, vaccine, biomarker, or treatment is proven
  without evidence level and subtype scope.
- Do not publish raw private data.
- Do not publish restricted data unless redistribution is allowed.
- Do not make tools that hide provenance or uncertainty.

## Preferred Output Shape

Every public biomedical artifact should answer:

- What question does this help with?
- Which blood-cancer subtype or mechanism is in scope?
- What sources support it?
- What does it not prove?
- Who should review it before action?
- How can someone reproduce or challenge it?
