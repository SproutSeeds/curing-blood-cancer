# Stewardship

This public surface is stewarded with the `frg.earth` mark so people can
connect the artifacts back to the broader work.

Stewardship means:

- keep public artifacts useful, source-backed, and reusable
- keep medical boundaries clear
- preserve provenance, uncertainty, and disease scope
- avoid patient-identifying data, private records, credentials, and restricted
  datasets
- make the public surface easier for researchers, patients, advocates, and
  builders to inspect and improve

## Stewardship Mark

Use this plain-text mark on public-facing repository pages and major artifacts:

```text
Stewarded by frg.earth
```

When a link is appropriate, use:

```markdown
Stewarded by [frg.earth](https://frg.earth/)
```

The mark is not a medical endorsement, treatment recommendation, or claim that
any cure has been found. It identifies stewardship of the public research
surface.
