# Cure Lab Public

## Scope

This file governs `/Volumes/Code_2TB/code/cure-lab-public`.

## Role

This is the public downstream repo for sanitized artifacts from the private
cure lab. It should be useful to the broader public, researchers, patients,
advocates, and builders.

## Boundary

- Public artifacts only.
- No patient-identifying data.
- No credentials, private records, restricted datasets, or unreviewed exports.
- No patient-specific medical advice.
- Every scientific or medical claim must carry provenance, scope, and
  uncertainty.

## Working Rules

- Make public artifacts readable without private context.
- Prefer reusable schemas, protocols, examples, and source-backed maps.
- Optimize for clarity, auditability, and contribution.
- Keep claims modest until evidence warrants stronger language.
