# Curing Blood Cancer

## Scope

This file governs `/Volumes/Code_2TB/code/curing-blood-cancer`.

## Role

This is the public downstream repo for sanitized Curing Blood Cancer artifacts
from the private lab. It should be useful to the broader public, researchers,
patients, advocates, and builders.

## Boundary

- Public artifacts only.
- No patient-identifying data.
- No credentials, private records, restricted datasets, or unreviewed exports.
- No patient-specific medical advice.
- Every scientific or medical claim must carry provenance, subtype scope, and
  uncertainty.
- Keep the repo blood-cancer-specific unless an adjacent mechanism is needed to
  explain leukemia, lymphoma, myeloma, MDS, MPN, marrow biology, immune therapy,
  or hematopoietic transplantation.

## Working Rules

- Make public artifacts readable without private context.
- Prefer reusable blood-cancer schemas, protocols, examples, and source-backed
  maps.
- Optimize for clarity, auditability, and contribution.
- Keep claims modest until evidence warrants stronger language.
